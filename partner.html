    JOIN AS PARTNER

    It has been around 5 years now since our Company started offering Coffee machines services at various Clubs, University, ARA’s etc and we must say it has been a very successful venture.

    The machines are installed and maintained by COFFEE SERVICES with the regular filling done by our contractor on regular basis. There is a general understanding in terms of commission charged by the site owner thereby creating a revenue for the Site owner as well. The price charged is most reasonable making it popular amongst students or at clubs. The process is very simple.

    We install the machine which also include a coin accepting option which enables the machine to offer beverages at a fixed price decided between the site owner and ourselves. We look after the machine including maintenance and topping up the ingredients including the complaints handling. If there is an arrangement with the site owner on the profit sharing, they get there due share each month ending.
