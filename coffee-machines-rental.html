    COFFEE MACHINES RENTAL

    Our Coffee/Tea machines (new or used) are available for any situation as per requirement of the Customer. The rent charged is very reasonable with the option of buying ingredients from us. There is a minimal contract period available depending upon the customer requirements.

    We have got comprehensive range of coffee/Tea machines available for rent including the fresh milk and instant coffee machines apart from the normal beans coffee machines. The equipment is available with or without permanent connection to the water mains and in different sizes depending upon the requirement.

    Please see our machines range and rent free machines range to know more about different types of Coffee & Tea machines we offer for rental.
